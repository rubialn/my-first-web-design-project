Trusted Websites & Learning Platforms

UN - Sustainable Development Goal 4 Overview

The official United Nations SDG 4 page gives a full overview of the goal, including the detailed targets and indicators used to measure progress on quality education. It presents global data, key challenges and updates on how countries are working to ensure inclusive and equitable learning for everyone. This is the best place to get an accurate, up-to-date picture of how the world is doing on SDG 4 straight from the UN.

Visit UN SDG 4 page

Global goals - Quality Education

The Global Goals website explains SDG 4 in a more interactive and youth-friendly way, using stories, visuals and simple language. It connects the goal to everyday life and suggests concrete actions that students, teachers and communities can take to support quality education locally. This platform is ideal if you want to be inspired, join campaigns or find ideas for classroom and youth projects linked to SDG 4.

Explore Global Goals site

UNESCO - Education

UNESCO’s education portal brings together global research, policy reports and data on how education systems are performing across different regions. It looks at issues like teacher training, curriculum quality, lifelong learning and equality of opportunity for learners of all ages. This site is especially useful if you want a deeper, more academic view of the challenges and solutions for achieving SDG 4 worldwide.

Browse UNESCO education

UNICEF -Education hub

The UNICEF education hub shows how education can transform children’s lives, with real stories, photos and case studies from different countries. It highlights the barriers that children face – such as conflict, gender inequality or poverty – and the projects that are helping them get into school and stay learning. You can also find advocacy materials and resources that support campaigns for every child’s right to a quality education.

Visit UNICEF education hub
